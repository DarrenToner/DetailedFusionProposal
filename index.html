Introduction

Thank you for considering Leviathan Studios Ltd. for the redevelopment of the Fusion Antibodies website. We are excited by the opportunity to build upon the strong foundation you have already established.

Our goal is to create a modern, fast, accessible, and SEO-friendly website that not only looks exceptional but also performs at the highest standards across all devices. We aim to preserve the integrity of your existing content, enhance your online presence, and ensure that the website is as future-proof as possible.

This document outlines the full scope of the project, including technical details, timelines, responsibilities, and costs, to ensure clarity and confidence at every stage of the process.

Scope of Work

This project will involve a full rebuild of the Fusion Antibodies website on a modern WordPress framework, while carefully retaining the existing content, structure, and essential functionalities.

The core scope of work includes:

  • Building a new custom theme to replace the current outdated theme.
  • Preserving existing content, imagery, and necessary plugins where appropriate.
  • Creating a dedicated landing page template for new services and market segments.
  • Ensuring best practices for mobile responsiveness, accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA), and SEO.
  • Optimising performance, including page load times and server response speeds.
  • Maintaining all necessary external links, including the Investor Hub, without interference.
  • Implementing a user-friendly CMS experience, allowing staff to update content with ease.

If access to the current website backend reveals additional technical dependencies or complexities, we will adapt the rebuild strategy accordingly. In rare cases where integrations are deeply tied to the current theme or plugins, a skeleton rebuild approach will be taken: stripping the site back to its essential structure and rebuilding outward while maintaining core functionality.

Project Responsibilities

The success of this project depends on clear responsibilities on both sides. Leviathan Studios Ltd will:

  • Design and develop a new, modern WordPress website using a custom-built theme and templates.
  • Migrate all existing content, posts, and media files into the new system where appropriate.
  • Implement SEO best practices, including basic metadata setup, heading structure, and sitemap creation.
  • Integrate Google Analytics 4 (GA4) tracking onto the site and assist with basic Search Console setup if required.
  • Carry out performance optimisations for speed and mobile responsiveness.
  • Provide one to two rounds of design revisions at the mock-up stage before development begins.
  • Ensure the new site is secure, mobile-friendly, and accessibility-aware.
  • Prepare a demonstration environment for client review prior to launch.
  • Provide staff training on updating and maintaining the website after handover.

Fusion Antibodies will be responsible for:

  • Providing logos, brand assets, preferred fonts or colours where available (or approving a provided style guide).
  • Supplying any updated content for pages and new landing pages if required.
  • Timely review and approval of designs, mock-ups, and development progress to keep the project on schedule.
  • Providing administrative access to the current WordPress site to facilitate migration work.
  • Providing domain access or assisting with DNS updates when the site is ready to go live.

SEO and Visibility Setup

As part of the rebuild, Leviathan Studios Ltd will ensure that the new website is prepared with strong SEO foundations to support search engine visibility and long-term growth. The following items are included:

  • Installation and setup of Rank Math SEO plugin (or a similar industry-standard tool).
  • Creation and submission of an XML sitemap to assist with indexing by search engines.
  • Setting appropriate metadata (titles and descriptions) for each core page.
  • Establishing a clear and correct heading structure across all pages (H1 to H6 hierarchy).
  • Implementation of 301 redirects if any page URLs are changed during the rebuild.
  • Adding structured Schema markup where relevant (e.g., organisation information, FAQs).
  • Basic optimisation of page URLs, ensuring they are descriptive and SEO friendly.
  • Internal linking between related pages where appropriate to strengthen SEO signals.
  • Advice and light assistance with initial keyword usage and page titles, where needed.
  • Installation of Google Analytics 4 (GA4) for tracking user engagement and performance.
  • Guidance on Google Search Console setup, or direct setup if required.

Please note: Leviathan Studios Ltd does not provide full SEO copywriting services as part of this proposal. However, light guidance on SEO best practices will be offered during the content and review phases.

Design and Development Approach

Leviathan Studios Ltd will deliver a full rebuild of the Fusion Antibodies website, focusing on modern design principles, flexibility, performance, and ease of management. Our approach is structured in clear stages to ensure transparency and collaboration throughout the project.

Initial Design Phase

  • Creation of a style guide (colours, typography, general brand tone) for client approval.
  • Design of key page mockups, such as the homepage, a service page, and a landing page template.
  • One to two rounds of revisions included to refine designs before development begins.
  • Client sign-off is required on designs before moving into development.

Development Phase

  • Development of a custom WordPress theme using modern best practices and a lightweight, future-proof builder.
  • Implementation of a flexible template for landing pages, allowing the client to easily create new pages without additional development.
  • Integration of client-provided content, or migration of existing content as placeholders where new content is pending.
  • Setup of clean, editor-friendly interfaces, allowing for easy updates through WordPress without technical expertise.
  • Careful preservation of any existing important functionality and external links, particularly the Investor Hub, which will be linked externally but not altered.

Accessibility and Best Practices

  • Attention to accessibility guidelines (contrast, font sizes, alt text) to promote an inclusive browsing experience.
  • Optimisation of site speed, including best practice caching, asset minification, and global content delivery via Cloudflare.

This approach ensures a professional, polished website that supports the business now and into the future, while allowing easy growth and changes as needed.

Content Migration Strategy

The transition to the new website will be handled with care to preserve the structure, SEO value, and usability of all existing content.

Scope of Migration

  • Migration of all current live pages, blog posts, media files, and downloadable documents.
  • Preservation of existing page URLs wherever possible to minimise disruption to SEO and user bookmarks.
  • Creation of 301 redirects where necessary, should any URLs change during the rebuild process.

Handling of Existing Content

  • All content will be treated as placeholders unless updated copy is provided during the project.
  • Large blocks of content will be restructured for improved readability and accessibility if needed, without altering the substance.
  • No rewriting of technical or scientific text will be undertaken without explicit client instruction, to ensure accuracy.

Landing Pages

  • We will create a flexible landing page template that can be duplicated and populated with tailored copy and images for each new service offering.
  • A flexible landing page template will be created, allowing for the addition of multiple new service pages or market-specific landing pages as needed.

This careful migration process ensures that the existing content remains a strong foundation while the new structure and design elevate the site’s overall performance and presentation.

Hosting, Launch, and Support

Hosting

  • The first year of hosting is included free of charge as part of the website rebuild project.
  • The site will be hosted on a dedicated UK-based server optimised for WordPress performance and security.
  • Following the first year, hosting will be billed monthly based on traffic and storage usage.
    Estimated cost for 250–350 monthly visitors: £30 per month (subject to review if website traffic grows significantly).

Launch Process

  • The rebuilt website will first be presented on a private demo server for review, feedback, and final approval.
  • DNS updates (or guidance) will be provided to smoothly transition the domain to the new server upon launch.
  • Pre-launch quality assurance checks will include device and browser testing, accessibility validation, and speed/performance verification.

Post-Launch Support

  • 30-day post-launch support window included for any minor adjustments or technical fixes required.
  • Ongoing maintenance included with hosting package: daily backups (7-day retention), plugin/theme updates, security monitoring, uptime monitoring, and server optimisation.
  • Up to 10 hours per month of minor content edits, technical support, or small development adjustments included as part of hosting service.
  • Additional major development requests beyond normal maintenance will be quoted separately.

Ownership

Upon final payment, full ownership of the completed website will transfer to Fusion Antibodies. This includes all custom themes, templates, and content developed specifically for the project.

The client will be provided with administrative access to the WordPress installation, allowing complete control over content management, plugin management, and future development if desired.

Leviathan Studios Ltd. retains no ongoing claim to the website once handed over, unless a separate maintenance or support agreement is in place.

Please note that while administrative access will be granted, clients are advised to use caution when making significant changes (such as installing new plugins or editing core settings). Any client-initiated changes made after handover that affect site functionality or performance are not covered under the initial build agreement. Ongoing support is available separately if required.

Additional Work and Change Requests

This proposal covers the full scope of work as outlined. Should additional features, major new pages, or substantial changes be requested after the initial agreement, these will be quoted and agreed separately before any additional work is undertaken.

Minor adjustments during the design and review stages (within the agreed rounds of revisions) are included. However, any significant changes to the project brief after approval of designs or commencement of development will be treated as additional work.

Examples of additional work may include, but are not limited to:

  • Development of custom functionality beyond the agreed scope
  • Creation of additional templates or complex page layouts
  • Integration of third-party systems not discussed during the initial brief
  • Extensive content rewriting, SEO copywriting, or strategy planning

Any additional work will be discussed openly, with clear pricing and timelines, before proceeding.

Exclusions

  • Extensive SEO copywriting or keyword research beyond basic on-page setup and structure
  • Content writing, editing, or rewriting of existing technical or scientific material
  • Custom software development, such as bespoke web applications or external platform integrations
  • CRM system integrations, complex form workflows, or third-party system connections outside of standard website functionality
  • Design or development of the external Investor Hub platform
  • Logo design, branding strategy, or brand identity services
  • Complex multilingual site setups (e.g., translations into multiple languages)
  • Third-party licensing fees (e.g., premium plugins, fonts, or stock imagery, unless otherwise specified)
  • Post-launch digital marketing campaigns, such as paid advertising or link building strategies

Should any of these items be required during or after the project, Leviathan Studios Ltd will be happy to provide a separate quotation.
